My name is Ethan Osborne. I am currently a Master's Student at SUNY Oswego studying Human-Computer Interaction. I am passionate about user-centered design and the processes behind it. I think users are the most important part of any design. Before I began studying HCI, I received my undergraduate degree in Psychology and worked for a local advertising agency, Pinckney Hugo Group with the express interest in becoming a web developer. After the internship concluded, I was searching for more work when I remembered that one of my Psychology professors had recommended looking into an HCI program. Fast forward to where I am now, entering my final semester at SUNY Oswego and passionate about helping create the best products utilizing user-centered design.
